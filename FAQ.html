Frequently Asked Questions

What is organ donation?

Organ donation is the process of giving an organ or a part of an organ to help someone in need of a transplant.

Who can become an organ donor?

Anyone can potentially become an organ donor regardless of age, medical history, or background. The medical condition at the time of death determines eligibility.

How can I register to become an organ donor?

You can register through your local government’s health department, organ donation websites, or by signing up when renewing your driver's license.

Does organ donation affect funeral arrangements?

No, organ donation does not affect funeral arrangements, and the donor’s body is treated with utmost respect and care.

Is there a cost for organ donation?

No, there are no costs to the donor’s family or estate for donating organs.
