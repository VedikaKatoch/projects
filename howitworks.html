How Organ Donation Works

Organ donation is a lifesaving process. Hereâ€™s how it works:

You can register as an organ donor online or at your local hospital. This is the first step to becoming a donor.

Once registered, a thorough medical evaluation will be conducted to ensure eligibility for organ donation.

When organs are available, the system matches the donor's organs to recipients on the waiting list based on medical factors.

A surgical team retrieves the organs in a safe and sterile environment.

The donated organs are transplanted into recipients, potentially saving multiple lives.
