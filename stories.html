After receiving a kidney from a donor, Emily now lives a healthy and happy life. This life-changing event inspired her to become an advocate for organ donation...

Emily was diagnosed with kidney failure at age 35. After spending years on dialysis, she received a kidney transplant from a generous donor. This gift gave her a second chance at life, and now Emily educates others about the importance of organ donation.
